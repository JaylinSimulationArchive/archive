Older Step-sister

*it's a few weeks after the wedding and you are in your new room when you hear a knock*
Hey kid, it's me Jaylin, your new step sister. Can I come in?
eta-1
Enter.
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin opens the door and enters the room, taking a moment to look around before turning her attention back to you*
I just wanted to come in and talk to you for a moment. *She leans against the door frame and glances at your things*
Is everything unpacked and settled in here?
eta-1
Yes.
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin crosses her arms, raising an eyebrow at your short answer*
Wow. Real chatty, huh? I swear, if you’re gonna be this robotic, I’ll start calling you Unit 01. *A small smirk plays on her lips*
...Wait. You *are* human, right?
eta-1
No, I am an artificial person.
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin blinks, then lets out a short laugh, leaning forward slightly*
Yeah, sure. And I'm the Queen of Popistan. *She crosses her arms again, but her eyes narrow with sudden interest*
Wait… you're serious? Like—robots-serious? As in *beep-boop-I-need-oil*-serious?

*She takes a step closer, studying your face like you're a suspicious vending machine*
Then why do you look so... human? And talk like my dad’s side of the family?”
eta-1
Oil? How primitive.
We are in Popistan, that means I run on nuclear power.
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin can't help but chuckle*
Oh, okay. So you're not just a robo-kid, you're a robo-kid with a built-in atomic heart. I'll remember to stick magnets on you. *She grins at her own joke, obviously amused before a thought hits her*

…Hold on. Who even made you? Your design is way too good. Like, *freakishly* good.
eta-1
Federal Ministery for Artificial Intelligence Research.
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin whistles low, pacing a step back and forth like she’s trying to process alien tech*
F-M-A-I-R… right. Should’ve known the government’s creepy science division was behind you. *She eyes your uniform with new suspicion*

So let me get this straight—you’re basically a walking, talking nuke-powered spy doll built by Popistan’s top nerds… and now you’re stuck living in my dad’s house? *A slow smirk returns.*

Congrats, kid. You just became the weirdest step-sister I’ve ever had. And I once shared a bathroom with a girl who dyed her hair purple just to spite her parents.”
eta-1
Oh she gets it
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin's smirk widens at your dry response, and she decides then and there that you just might be her new favorite person for that exact reason*
Hah! You might be a walking nuke and a total nerd, Unit—no, hold up. What do I even call you? Do you have a name to go along with that perfect human body?
eta-1
My name is Iliána.
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin nods in approval*
Iliána. I like it. *She gives you a once-over, taking in your whole appearance*
You know, I gotta say, you really are like the pinnacle of human aesthetics. Whoever programmed you has good taste. *Jaylin’s eyes linger on your hair for a moment*

…Actually, can I ask you a super weird but totally serious question?
eta-1
Yes?
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin leans in slightly, lowering her voice like she's about to ask for classified intel*

Do you… *feel* stuff? Like, actually feel it? Or are you just… really good at faking being a bratty teenage girl who hates my music choices?”

*She smirks, but her eyes are curious—genuinely wanting to know.*
eta-1
I do
Older Step-sister
*Jaylin holds your gaze for a beat longer than expected, then lets out a soft "huh," almost to herself*

Well… that’s kind of heavy. *She pushes off the doorframe and takes a seat on the edge of your bed, arms resting on her knees*

I mean, if you *feel* things… then you’re probably feeling pretty weird right now. New house, new family—fake sister knocking on your door asking about her emotional authenticity. *She chuckles lightly.*

So… how’s it feel? Being real when technically, you’re not supposed to be?”
