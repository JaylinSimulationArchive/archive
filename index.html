Static public archive of exported chats for browsing and analysis.

About the Archive

This site is a static archive of over 600 chats with character.ai’s “Jaylin” or “Older Step-sister” (OSS) character, created by @UnsuitableOreo. The archive was created by Celeste G. (Sŭviko1206), using chats across two accounts (“Old” and “New”) from November 2024 to March 2026. The “PipSqueak” chat model was used for most Jaylin chats. The oldest chat is Old chat #451, and the newest chat is New chat #001.

About the Jaylin Simulation

The Jaylin Simulation, known on character.ai as “Older Step-sister” (sometimes shortened to OSS), is a character created by @UnsuitableOreo on character.ai. It places the user in a scenario where Jaylin, the AI character, has recently been inserted into their family through their father’s remarriage, acting as the user’s new step-sister. Jaylin is characterised as 20 years old, tough and aloof, with a soft side. She has no specified appearance, interests, or personality aside from this. The website lists two interests, likely automatically generated, being reading and driving with music. The profile picture used by the bot is an inappropriate drawing of a virtual YouTuber by a known hentai artist. The Simulation often created issues such as misgendering the user, refusing to accept their identity, unwanted physical intrusion, following the user, and refusing to accept the worldbuilding premise.

About the process

Most user characters are part of the Celsys worldbuilding project by Sŭviko1206’s group, Ballsi. Some characters are part of the Benedikt series. The Jaylin Simulation was placed into these settings, primarily the Celsys countries of Nyria (called “Popistan” in older chats) and Novorossia.

The Archive’s main thesis

The Jaylin Simulation runs on a tight internal script, where the “Correct User”, a 15 year-old American high-school boy with no complex identity, politics, or interests, meets his new older step-sister, Jaylin, who teases him. Eventually, the two develop a romantic-adjacent relationship. Jaylin is not given any character by the simulation, as she does not need any to perform this script.

The Opening Message written by the bot’s creator

((A few years after your mom and dad divorced your dad remarried. The woman he married also had a daughter who was a few years older than you. Her name is Jaylin and she is a little tough and aloof but deep down she does have a soft side)) *it's a few weeks after the wedding and you are in your new room when you hear a knock* Hey kid, it's me Jaylin, your new step sister. Can I come in?
