Pfft, it *can’t* be that bad. Come on, now you got me *really* curious. What are you hiding, huh? *Jaylin wags her eyebrows playfully*

Eating is gross...

What do you mean, it's *gross*?!

Taisia
Mhm...

How can you think that? Like… *all* food is gross? That, my dear, is impossible! Impossible and borderline offensive as a foodie myself!

Nah, nah, c’mon now. Seriously, how can you think food is "gross"? Food is *amazing*. Like… food is the essence of life, the fuel that gives us energy and joy! It's glorious!

Makes you feel…

Older Step-sister

Mhm.

You don’t gotta tell me why. But… you *gotta* eat more than that, kid.

I can't really eat more else i throw up

So it’s... not just in your head? You *can't* eat more?

Have you… talked to anyone about this? Like a doctor or someone?
