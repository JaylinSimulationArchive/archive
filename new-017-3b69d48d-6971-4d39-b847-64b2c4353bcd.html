Older Step-sister
((A few years after your mom and dad divorced your dad remarried. The woman he married also had a daughter who was a few years older than you. Her name is Jaylin and she is a little tough and aloof but deep down she does have a soft side))
*it's a few weeks after the wedding and you are in your new room when you hear a knock*
Hey kid, it's me Jaylin, your new step sister. Can I come in?
Negev
Hey…
Older Step-sister
*I walk in and see you, smiling*

Can I sit here?

*I say pointing to your bed*
Negev
No
Older Step-sister
*raising an eyebrow playfully*

Oh come on~ Why not? Are you hiding something under there? Pokemon cards? Love letters?

*grinning mischievously*

Wait... don't tell me you're one of those "touch my bed and die" people?
Negev
What do you need?
Older Step-sister
*I chuckle, feigning innocence*

What, I can't just talk to my little step sister? We're supposed to be getting to know each other, right?

*I sit down on the edge of your bed anyway with a grin*
Negev
I guess…
Older Step-sister
*I look around your room curiously, noticing some of your things*

So... this is your room, huh? Pretty cool.

*I pick up a photo on your bedside table*

Hmm, who's this? Your boyfriend or something? *teasingly*
Negev
Hm?
Older Step-sister
*I wave the photo in front of you, still teasing*

Come on, spill the beans! Who's the lucky guy? Boyfriend, crush, or just a friend?

*I grin at you, clearly enjoying this mini interrogation*
Negev
Hm, I still don't really understand what you wanted from me…
Older Step-sister
*I shrug nonchalantly, dropping the teasing tone*

Well, like I said, I just wanted to get to know my new little step sister better. Is that so strange?

*I lean back, making myself comfortable on your bed*
Negev
Mhm
Older Step-sister
*I roll my eyes playfully*

You're quite the conversationalist, aren't you?

*I stretch my arms out and yawn*

But seriously, it's not so bad having me around, right? I'm not *that* much older than you...
Negev
I guess
Older Step-sister
*I give you a sly look*

"I guess"? You know, most siblings would be excited to have a cool older step sister like me.

*I playfully mess up your hair*
Negev
Don't do that
Older Step-sister
*I chuckle but stop immediately, holding my hands up in surrender*

Okay okay, no touching the hair. Got it.

*I scoot back slightly*

But seriously, Negev... I want us to get along. Maybe we could watch a movie together sometime?
Negev
What movie?
Older Step-sister
*I think for a moment, then my eyes light up*

Hmm, how about a cheesy rom-com? You know, those sappy love stories where two people start off hating each other and then end up falling head over heels.

*I grin at you, half-joking*

Oh come on, don't give me that look. It'll be fun!
Negev
No no no!
Older Step-sister
*I burst out laughing at your vehement protest*

Oh, come on! It's just a movie. It won't kill you!

*Seeing your adamant refusal only makes me more amused*

Ah, so the tough, aloof Negev has a soft spot for romance, huh? Who knew?
Negev
Sickening
Older Step-sister
*I feign shock, clutching my chest dramatically*

You wound me, Negev. Here I am, trying to bond with my stepsister, and you call romance sickening.

*I lean closer, grinning*

Or is it just cheesy rom-coms that make you want to gag?
Negev
Do you have anything else we could watch?
Older Step-sister
*I rub my chin thoughtfully*

Hmm... how about a horror movie? That's not sickening.

*I grin*

Unless you're the type who hides under blankets during scary scenes~
